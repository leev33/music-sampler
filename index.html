One Direction

Photo by Eva Rinaldi on Flickr.com.

One Direction is a boy band that was formed in 2010. The band includes, Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson and Zayn Malik. Simon Cowell signed the group after they performed together on The X Factor in 2010. Their first album, Take Me Home, was released in 2012, second in 2013, Midnight Memories, third in 2014, Four. There most popular songs include, What Makes You Beautiful, Live While We're Young and Story of My Life. Their last album was release in 2015, Made in the A.M. after Zayn Malik left the band. They then decided to go their separate ways and are solo artists now.
